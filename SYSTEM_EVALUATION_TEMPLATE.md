## Scoring Reference

### Point Distribution:
- **UI Category:** 25 points (5 items × 5 points each)
- **Functionality Category:** 25 points (5 items × 5 points each)
- **Analytics Category:** 25 points (5 items × 5 points each)
- **Total Possible Score:** 75 points

### Overall Rating Calculation:
- Divide total score by 15 (75 ÷ 5 = 15, then divide by 3 categories)
- Or average the three category ratings (each out of 5)

### Rating Scale:
- **1.0 - 1.9** = Poor (Needs significant improvement)
- **2.0 - 2.9** = Below Average (Needs improvement)
- **3.0 - 3.9** = Average (Acceptable)
- **4.0 - 4.9** = Good (Above average)
- **5.0** = Excellent (Outstanding)
