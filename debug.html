If you see "Missing environment variables":

  1. Go to your Vercel dashboard
  2. Select your CRMS project
  3. Go to Settings â†’ Environment Variables
  4. Add these variables:

    • VITE_NEON_DATABASE - Your database name
    • VITE_NEON_USER - Your Neon username
    • VITE_NEON_PASSWORD - Your Neon password
    • VITE_NEON_PORT - 5432 (default)
  5. Redeploy: vercel --prod
